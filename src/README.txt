particleFlow is app simulating behaviour of particles in relation with each other and obstacles.
The closer particle color is to red, the faster that particle is relatively to rest of particles.

To create obstacle click left mouse button and drag it. When you release device will be created.
You can remove obstacles by clicking on them.
You can change obstacle type, by scrolling your mouse wheel.

First number in config defines size of window and second density of particles on the map.
